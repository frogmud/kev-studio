Questions & Answers

Is there parking available at the venue?

Yes! Valet parking is complimentary at Hotel LBI for our guests.

Can I bring a guest or children?

Although we would love to accommodate everyone who wants to come and celebrate with us, we can only accommodate those noted on the invitation.

Is there a dress code?

Formal attire.

Where can I stay the night?

We have room blocks available at Hotel LBI and Holiday Inn Manahawkin/Long Beach Island. View accommodation details.

Can we check in early at our hotel?

Check-in time is 4:00 PM and early check-ins are not guaranteed. The hotel can securely hold your luggage prior to check-in.
